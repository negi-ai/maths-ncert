Class 11 Maths

Chapter 1: Sets

Q1: If A = {1,2,3} and B = {2,3,4}, find A ∪ B.

A: A ∪ B = {1,2,3,4}

Chapter 2: Relations and Functions

Q1: Determine if f(x) = x² is one-one.

A: Not one-one as f(2) = f(-2) = 4

Chapter 3: Trigonometric Functions

Q1: Find sin 30° + cos 60°.

A: sin30° + cos60° = 1/2 + 1/2 = 1

Chapter 4: Principle of Mathematical Induction

Q1: Prove 1 + 2 + … + n = n(n+1)/2

A: Base case: n=1 → 1=1(1+1)/2 =1 ✔. Assume true for n=k, prove for n=k+1.

Chapter 5: Complex Numbers and Quadratic Equations

Q1: Solve x² + 1 = 0

A: x² = -1 → x = i, -i

Chapter 6: Linear Inequalities

Q1: Solve x - 3 < 2

A: x < 5

Chapter 7: Permutations and Combinations

Q1: How many ways to arrange 3 letters from A, B, C?

A: 3! = 6 ways

Chapter 8: Binomial Theorem

Q1: Expand (a+b)³

A: a³ + 3a²b + 3ab² + b³

Chapter 9: Sequences and Series

Q1: Find the sum of first 10 natural numbers

A: n(n+1)/2 = 10*11/2 = 55

Chapter 10: Straight Lines

Q1: Find equation of line through (1,2) with slope 3

A: y - 2 = 3(x - 1) → y = 3x -1

Chapter 11: Conic Sections

Q1: Equation of circle with center (0,0) and radius 5

A: x² + y² = 25

Chapter 12: Introduction to Three Dimensional Geometry

Q1: Distance between points P(1,2,3) and Q(4,5,6)

A: √[(4-1)² + (5-2)² + (6-3)²] = √27 = 3√3

Chapter 13: Limits and Derivatives

Q1: Find lim(x→0) sinx/x

A: 1

Chapter 14: Mathematical Reasoning

Q1: If p → q is false, what are p and q?

A: p = True, q = False

Chapter 15: Statistics

Q1: Find mean of 2,4,6,8,10

A: (2+4+6+8+10)/5 = 6

Chapter 16: Probability

Q1: Probability of getting head in coin toss

A: 1/2
